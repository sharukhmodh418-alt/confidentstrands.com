About Hair Patches

What Is a Hair Patch?

A hair patch is a custom-made, non-surgical hair replacement system crafted from 100% real human hair. It is carefully fitted to your scalp to seamlessly blend with your existing hair, giving you a completely natural look that nobody can tell apart from real hair.

Unlike transplants, there's no pain, no scarring, and no recovery time. You walk in with thinning hair and walk out with a full, styled head of hair — all in under 2 hours.
